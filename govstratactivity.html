Read each scenario and select the strategy that would work best to address the boundary issue. Think about what would most directly address the root cause of the problem.

The Five Strategies:

  1. Provide comprehensive reporting - Give board regular, detailed reports
  2. Create space for questions - Include time for board to ask about operations
  3. Establish feedback mechanism - Create respectful way to signal boundary crossings
  4. Provide regular training - Offer ongoing education with scenarios
  5. Put it in writing - Document boundaries in bylaws and policies
